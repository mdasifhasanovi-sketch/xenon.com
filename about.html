Our Story

About Xenon Industries

Creating real-life robots inspired by science fiction

Our Mission

Xenon Industries is a Bangladeshi startup dedicated to creating innovative desk robots that enhance productivity and well-being. We believe technology should be calming, expressive, and human-centered.

Our flagship product, PikoBot, represents our commitment to designing tools that help people focus, stay mindful, and work in a more intentional way.

Our Vision

We envision a world where robots are not just tools, but companions that understand the importance of a calm workspace. Our robots communicate without voice, intrude without demanding attention, and help you maintain focus through gentle guidance.

Every design decision is made with the user's mental well-being in mind, creating a more human workspace inspired by science fiction.
